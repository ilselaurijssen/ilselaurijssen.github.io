Research profile
======
Sociologist specialising in educational inequality, longitudinal data analysis and large-scale educational data infrastructures. Currently data analysis coordinator in the Steunpunt Centrale Toetsen in Onderwijs, which is a Flemish policy support centre responsible for the development and analysis of central assessments of academic skills in primary and secondary education (“Vlaamse toetsen”).

My research focuses on the interpretation and responsible use of educational performance indicators, combining advanced quantitative methods with sociological perspectives on educational policy and inequality. I have extensive experience in the development, management and analysis of longitudinal survey data, administrative datasets and population-wide educational assessments. My work is embedded in large-scale policy-oriented research programmes, where I coordinate research activities and collaborate within inter-university and interdisciplinary research teams.
