Courses taught:

* Sociology (“Sociologie”, 3 ECTS). KULeuven, Campus Brussel, 2024-2025. 
  * Introductory course on sociology for first bachelor students in Law, including students from joint multilingual Bachelor in Law program organised by UCLouvain Saint-Louis Brussels and KULeuven.
* Sociology of Time and the Time Order (“Sociologie van de tijd en de tijdsordening”, 6 ECTS), Vrije Universiteit Brussel, 2018-2019. 
  * Lectures, seminars and guest lecturers; day and evening classes for working students.
* Advanced Data Analysis for the Social Sciences (“Gevorderde data-analyse voor de sociale wetenschappen”, 6 ECTS, shared with Dr. Jessy Siongers), Vrije Universiteit Brussel, 2012-2013. 
  * Lectures and computer lab sessions; day and evening classes for working students.

Additional teaching activities:

* Sociology of Social Equality and Inequality (“Sociologie van sociale gelijkheid en ongelijkheid”; Prof. Ignace Glorieux, Laura Van den Borre 2018-2019; Deborah De Moortel 2020-2021), Vrije Universiteit Brussel
  * guest lecture each year 2004-2021, on social inequality in education, educational careers of migrant youth, integration of migrant youth into the labour market.
* Seminar Sociology I, Vrije Universiteit Brussel, 2013-2014
  * evaluation and provision of feedback to first bachelor students about their seminar paper on social inequality.
* Sociological Workshop: About Exclusion and Social Integration (“Sociologisch atelier: Over exclusie en maatschappelijke integratie”, Prof. Jacques Tacq), Hogeschool Universiteit Brussel, 2009-2010
  * guest lecture on the educational careers and integration of youth of migrant descent into the labour market.
*	Supervision of bachelor and master theses
*	Participation in master thesis and PhD examination committees
